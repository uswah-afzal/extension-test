# Onix Meeting Assistant - First Time User Guide

## 🎓 What to Expect When Using Onix for Your First Class

### Step 1: Opening the Extension
1. **Join your class** (Google Meet or Zoom)
2. **Click the Onix extension icon** in your Chrome toolbar
3. The **side panel opens** on the right side of your screen

### Step 2: Sign In (First Time Only)
- You'll see: **"Not signed in"** message
- Click **"Sign in"** button
- Choose your Google account
- Grant permissions
- ✅ You're now signed in!

### Step 3: Starting Your Class Capture
1. **Enter meeting title** (optional) - e.g., "Math 101 - Lecture 5"
2. Click **"Start Capture"** button
3. **What happens:**
   - Status changes to: "Capturing and saving..."
   - Transcript area starts showing live text
   - You'll see speaker names and what they're saying in real-time
   - Extension is now recording everything!

### Step 4: During Your Class (What You See)

#### **Live Transcript (Automatic)**
- **Real-time transcription** appears in the transcript box
- Shows: `Speaker Name: What they said [Time]`
- Auto-scrolls so you always see the latest
- **No action needed** - it just works!

#### **Auto-Save (Automatic)**
- Transcript saves automatically every 15 seconds
- You'll see: "✓ Auto-saved to Firestore" message
- **No action needed** - it just works!

#### **Auto-Notes Generation (Automatic)**
- Every 2 minutes, notes are automatically generated
- You'll see: "📝 Auto-generated X note(s)" message
- Notes include:
  - 💡 Key concepts
  - 📖 Definitions
  - ⭐ Important points
  - 📚 Examples
  - ❓ Questions
- **No action needed** - it just works!

#### **Paste Image (Optional)**
- When you see an important slide/diagram:
  1. Take a screenshot manually (Windows: Win+Shift+S, Mac: Cmd+Shift+4)
  2. Click **"📋 Paste Image"** button
  3. Paste your screenshot (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V)
  4. Optional: Add a caption (e.g., "Important formula")
  5. Click **"Save"**
  6. Done! Image saved with your note

### Step 5: Ending Your Class
1. Click **"Stop Capture"** button
2. **What happens automatically:**
   - Final transcript is saved
   - Summary is generated (if transcript > 200 chars)
   - Action items are extracted
   - Status shows: "✓ Final transcript saved"
   - Summary generation starts in background

### Step 6: Viewing Your Notes on Dashboard

1. **Go to dashboard**: `http://localhost:3000` (or your dashboard URL)
2. **Sign in** with same Google account
3. **Click "Meetings"** in sidebar
4. **Click on your class** from the list

#### **You'll See 3 Tabs:**

##### **Tab 1: Transcript**
- Full transcript of entire class
- Everything that was said
- Organized chronologically

##### **Tab 2: Summary / Action Items**
- **Summary Section:**
  - AI-generated summary of the class
  - Key discussion points
  - Decisions made
  - Next steps
  
- **Action Items Section:**
  - Tasks extracted from the class
  - Who they're assigned to (if mentioned)
  - Due dates (if mentioned)

##### **Tab 3: Notes**
- **Auto-Generated Notes** (every 5 minutes):
  - 💡 Concepts (purple badge)
  - 📖 Definitions (yellow badge)
  - ⭐ Key Points (orange badge)
  - 📚 Examples (indigo badge)
  - ❓ Questions (red badge)
  
- **Manual Screenshots** (when you captured):
  - 📷 Screenshot image
  - Your caption (if added)
  - Timestamp
  
- **All organized chronologically** - see the flow of your class!

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to take notes manually?**  
A: No! Notes are generated automatically every 2 minutes.

**Q: What if I miss something?**  
A: Everything is transcribed, so you can review the full transcript later.

**Q: Can I edit the notes?**  
A: Currently notes are auto-generated, but you can add captions to screenshots.

**Q: How long does summary generation take?**  
A: Usually 10-30 seconds after you stop the meeting.

**Q: How do I add images to my notes?**  
A: Take a screenshot manually (Win+Shift+S or Cmd+Shift+4), then click "Paste Image" and paste it (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V).
